For trucks, SUVs, or vans, a traditional cable kit may not cut it after dark in a remote area. Our verdict

The best car emergency kit doesn‘t have to be the most expensive one, it just has to cover the emergencies you’re most likely to face. For the broadest protection, the AAA Emergency Roadside Kit gets our top recommendation. If you drive a large vehicle or want a standalone jump pack, the Stanley Fatmax is the premium pick, and the Energizer kit is a smart, no-fuss choice for anyone on a budget. Whichever you choose, keeping it in your trunk is the cheapest insurance you‘ll ever buy.
